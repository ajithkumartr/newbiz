About Us

Welcome to the first edition of the NewBiz !

NewBiz was founded in 2024 by a group of passionate recent graduates with diverse backgrounds in engineering, design, and business. United by a shared vision, we set out to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and everyday usability. Our team is dedicated to creating products that make a real difference in the lives of our customers.

Our Vision

At NewBiz, our vision is to revolutionize everyday life through innovative, affordable, and easy-to-use robotics. We believe that advanced technology should be accessible to everyone, enhancing convenience, efficiency, and quality of life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to design and manufacture user-friendly robots that seamlessly integrate into the daily routines of households and businesses. We aim to democratize robotics, making high-quality solutions available to the common man at an affordable price.

Community Involvement

At NewBiz, we are committed to giving back to the community. We actively participate in local events and educational workshops to promote STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education. By partnering with schools and community organizations, we provide hands-on learning experiences and mentorship programs to inspire the next generation of innovators.

We also focus on making technology accessible to underserved communities. Through collaborations with non-profit organizations, we donate robots and tech resources to schools and community centers, ensuring everyone can benefit from our innovations. Our outreach programs aim to bridge the digital divide and empower individuals with the skills they need in a technology-driven world.

Our Journey

NewBiz began in 2024 when a group of passionate recent graduates united with a shared vision to make advanced robotics accessible and affordable for everyone. Starting from a small rented office, we faced typical startup challenges but persevered by winning a local startup competition and securing seed funding. Our focus on extensive research and user feedback led to the development of our flagship product, the HomeBot, designed to simplify household chores.

Over the years, NewBiz has grown from a humble startup into a thriving organization with a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility. Key milestones include the successful pilot testing of the HomeBot, the expansion of our product line, and the launch of pre-orders. Our dedicated team continues to innovate and drive our mission forward, making significant strides in the world of consumer robotics.

We invite you to join us on our journey to bring smart solutions to every home. Follow us on social media, sign up for our newsletter, and stay updated on the latest news and product releases from NewBiz.
