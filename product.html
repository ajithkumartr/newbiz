Our Products

Discover our range of smart robots designed to make your everyday tasks easier and more enjoyable.

HomeBot

Price Range: CAD 299 - 399

The Ultimate Home Assistant. Effortlessly handle various household chores with automated cleaning, voice control, and advanced sensors.

Features:

  • Automated Cleaning: Efficiently vacuums and dusts floors and surfaces.
  • Voice Control: Simple voice commands for easy operation.
  • Advanced Sensors: Navigates around furniture and obstacles safely.
  • Energy Efficient: Low power consumption and eco-friendly design.

Functions:

HomeBot is equipped with intelligent cleaning algorithms, allowing it to detect dirt and adjust its cleaning path. It also has a scheduling feature, so you can set it to clean at specific times, ensuring your home is always spotless without any effort on your part.

GardenBot

Price Range: CAD 399 - 499

Your Smart Gardening Companion. Automates lawn care, waters plants, and monitors soil conditions for a vibrant garden with minimal effort.

Features:

  • Automated Lawn Care: Mows the lawn and trims edges with precision.
  • Plant Care: Waters plants and monitors soil conditions.
  • Weather-Resistant: Designed to withstand various weather conditions.
  • Intuitive Scheduling: Easily set and adjust care schedules.

Functions:

GardenBot is designed to keep your garden in top shape. It uses smart sensors to assess soil moisture and nutrient levels, providing the necessary care for your plants. Its weather-resistant build ensures reliable performance in any condition, and its easy-to-use app allows you to customize and monitor its activities from anywhere.

CareBot

Price Range: CAD 499 - 599

Personal Assistance for the Elderly. Provides support with medication reminders, emergency alerts, and daily tasks to ensure safety and comfort.

Features:

  • Medication Reminders: Alerts users to take their medication on time.
  • Emergency Alerts: Sends alerts to caregivers in case of emergencies.
  • Daily Assistance: Helps with daily tasks and routines.
  • Empathetic AI: Interacts with users in a friendly and supportive manner.

Functions:

CareBot is tailored to support the elderly and individuals with special needs. It offers reliable reminders for medications and appointments, and its emergency alert system ensures help is just a call away. The empathetic AI provides companionship and assistance, enhancing the user's quality of life.

WareBot

Price Range: CAD 999 - 1299

Optimizing Warehouses and Manufacturing. Streamlines inventory management, automates repetitive tasks, and enhances operational efficiency.

Features:

  • Automated Inventory Management: Tracks and manages stock levels in real-time.
  • Task Automation: Handles repetitive tasks such as picking, packing, and sorting.
  • High Load Capacity: Can transport heavy items efficiently.
  • Integration Capabilities: Seamlessly integrates with existing warehouse management systems.

Functions:

WareBot is designed to improve efficiency in warehouses and manufacturing environments. It automates inventory tracking, reducing human error and ensuring accurate stock levels. Its robust build and high load capacity make it ideal for handling heavy items, while its integration capabilities allow it to work seamlessly with your existing systems.
