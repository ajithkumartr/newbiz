Our History

Welcome to the first edition of the NewBiz !

NewBiz was born out of a shared vision among a group of recent graduates who recognized the potential of robotics to transform everyday life. United by their passion for technology and innovation, they set out to create an organization dedicated to making advanced robotics accessible and affordable for everyone.

Founding

In early 2024, the founding team of NewBiz came together with a bold mission: to develop user-friendly robots that could seamlessly integrate into the daily lives of the common man. With diverse backgrounds in engineering, design, and business, the team pooled their knowledge and expertise to bring this vision to life.

Early Development

The initial phase of NewBiz's journey was marked by extensive research and development. The team focused on identifying the key challenges faced by households and brainstorming robotic solutions that could address these issues. Their efforts led to the creation of the HomeBot, a versatile household robot designed to handle various chores with ease.

Initial Challanges & Breakthroughs

The early days of NewBiz were filled with challenges typical of a startup. The team worked out of a small rented office, juggling various roles and responsibilities. Securing initial funding was a major hurdle, but their persistence paid off when they won a local startup competition, which provided seed money and valuable mentorship.

During this phase, the team focused on extensive market research to understand the needs and pain points of potential customers. They discovered that many existing robots were either too expensive or too complex for the average household. This insight guided their product development process.

Growth & Expansion

Following the successful prototype of the HomeBot, NewBiz secured initial funding from angel investors who believed in the transformative potential of the companyâ€™s vision. This funding allowed NewBiz to scale up production, hire additional talent, and establish a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility.

As the company grew, NewBiz also established partnerships with key suppliers and technology providers to ensure the highest quality components for their robots. These partnerships have been crucial in maintaining the affordability of their products without compromising on quality.

Looking ahead, NewBiz aims to continue innovating and expanding its product offerings. The company is exploring international markets and developing robots tailored to specific regional needs. With a strong foundation and a clear mission, NewBiz is poised to make a significant impact in the world of consumer robotics.
